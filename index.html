The first step was the born of the desire "we want to provide a moment of peace in everyone's day". after many conversations we found that nature = peace so here SereneBite was founded.

In 2018 Summer finally the time has come for SereneBite to welcome all who aims for a tranquil oasis for nature enthusiasts. It's designed to bring the outdoors inside, with plenty of indoor plants, wooden decor, and large windows for natural light.

We're going to open the doors of the second branch of SereneBite in North London. Stay Tuned!

our goal is Creating a serene haven where nature and gourmet dining harmonize, offering an escape from the hustle and bustle of daily life.

Natural Haven
SereneBite Cafe is a tranquil oasis for nature enthusiasts. It's designed to bring the outdoors inside, with plenty of indoor plants, wooden decor, and large windows for natural light.

Relaxation Zones
The cafe is divided into different relaxation zones, from cosy corners with bookshelves to a sunlit conservatory filled with
lush plants.

Gourmet Health
The cafe prides itself on serving health-conscious gourmet food and beverages, using locally sourced, organic ingredients.

Outdoor Deck
SereneBite offers an outdoor deck for customers to enjoy their meals in the open air surrounded by greenery.

Books reading
  • weakly event
  • most popular

Join us for a tranquil literary journey at SereneBite Cafe's Books Reading event. Immerse yourself in the soothing ambiance of our nature-inspired cafe as we gather to enjoy the company of fellow book lovers. Whether you're a seasoned reader or just looking for a quiet escape, our book readings offer a unique blend of serenity and storytelling. Snuggle up in our cosy corners, sip on nature-infused beverages, and let the words transport you to new worlds.

Latte Art Basics
  • monthly event
  • limited Participants
  • most popular

Discover the artistry of coffee at SereneBite Cafe's Latte Art Basics event. Our skilled baristas will guide you through the magical process of creating beautiful latte art designs. Perfect for coffee enthusiasts and budding baristas, this hands-on experience allows you to turn your latte into a canvas. Learn the secrets of pouring delicate hearts, leaves, and more, all while savouring our gourmet, health-conscious brews. Unleash your creativity.

Nature Walks
  • twice a week
  • most popular

Experience the tranquility of nature at SereneBite Cafe's Nature Walks Event. Step outside our doors and embark on a guided journey through the nearby park's lush greenery. Our expert guides will lead you on a captivating exploration of the natural world, sharing the secrets of the local flora and fauna. This event is your opportunity to escape the urban hustle and connect with the serene beauty of the outdoors. Join us and embrace the soothing sights and sounds of nature.

Gardening Basics
  • monthly event
  • limited Participants

Unleash your inner gardener at SereneBite Cafe's Gardening Basics event. Our community garden is the perfect setting for hands-on learning. Discover the joy of nurturing plants, as our gardening experts share essential tips and techniques. From selecting the right soil to planting and caring for your green companions, you'll gain the skills needed to cultivate your own little oasis.

Yoga and Meditation Sessions
  • monthly event

Regular yoga and meditation classes are offered in the tranquil garden, helping You find inner peace.
